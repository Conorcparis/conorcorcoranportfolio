Professional Journey

Australian Experience

In Melbourne, I managed a number of well-known Irish pubs before traveling the country for six months. Then in 2005, back to France, armed with lots of new ideas—notably screw-top wine and pubs. I saw the pubs zero wine waste.

Global Beverage Distribution

2005 - 2006

Between 2005 and 2006, I supported boutique wineries in France with market development in Europe, the US, and Asia. I organized focused B2B events matching buyers and sellers, which led me to realize that a screw-cap French wine in the on-trade in France, marketed in English, was a great idea. It was: we ended up selling over 300,000 bottles over 10 years, with zero returns.

Entrepreneurial Growth with Direct Distribution

2006 - 2020

In 2006, I co-founded Direct Distribution, a network for premium beverages and snacks. We started with 20 products and €45,000 in seed funding and grew annual revenue to €2 million within 6 years. By 2019, the portfolio expanded to 210 SKUs and turnover reached €2.4 million, with an average gross margin of 28%. My roles spanned warehouse layout optimization, demand forecasting, and key account management.

Digital Transformation During COVID-19

2020 - 2024

When the pandemic hit in March 2020, I led our pivot to a consumer-facing e-commerce platform. I oversaw the development of a custom CRM and Shopify storefront, implemented a machine-learning model that reduced inventory shortfalls by 15%, and launched marketing automation that increased email engagement by 10%.

Transition to AI-Powered SaaS

With certifications in Python and machine learning—and a recent project building of AI agents for SMEs —I'm poised to blend data science with my considerable sales, marketing and cross border project management and team building.
