Professional Journey

I'm a growth-focused leader who's navigated the full arc of business—from Big Four consulting and financial services to scaling niche import ventures and leading direct-to-consumer transformations. Today, I'm blending that experience with next-gen tools to shape the future of AI-powered business.

My career began at PwC Dublin, where I worked as a junior consultant in import/export classification for computer components. After a year in consulting, I transitioned into full-time academics—earning a BSc in Management from Trinity College Dublin and a Diploma in Marketing from DIT. I immersed myself in MBA-level disciplines including corporate strategy, financial modeling, operations management, marketing analytics, organizational behavior, and competitive market positioning. A six-month placement in financial services gave me hands-on experience advising clients on mortgages and investment products.

In 2000, I relocated to France to refine my language skills and expand my international business experience. By 2001, I was managing key accounts and trade marketing for Brasseries Kronenbourg. Over the next few years, I successfully launched Beamish stout as a challenger to Guinness and played a pivotal role in putting Carlsberg and Foster's on the map in France, securing long-term volume contracts and national placements.

In 2004, I moved to Australia to deepen my understanding of supplier ecosystems and global trends. I identified an opportunity to reintroduce Anglo-Irish specialty beverages to the French market. That insight led to a year in business development for boutique wineries across the U.S. and Europe.

In 2006, I founded a direct-distribution network and scaled it over 15 years from a handful of SKUs to a €30M+ turnover, managing portfolios in wine, beer, cider, spirits, and snacks while leading on logistics, compliance, brand strategy, and retail execution.

When the pandemic hit, I led a rapid transformation from B2B distribution to a consumer-facing e-commerce model—developing a custom CRM, AI-driven forecasting engine, and automated marketing infrastructure to fuel growth and resilience in uncertain times.

Recently, I've been refocusing on upskilling in AI development, prompt engineering, and software innovation—bringing things full circle with a return to the SaaS space I first explored in 1996. With a career built on strategic insight, operational execution, and technical fluency, I'm now positioned to lead in the next wave of AI-native digital transformation.
