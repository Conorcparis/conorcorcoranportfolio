Professional Journey

I'm a growth-focused leader who's navigated the full spectrum of business—from Big Four consulting and banking to niche importers and direct-to-consumer ventures.

After a year at PwC in Dublin as a junior consultant in their import-export practice, I dove into academics in 1996, earning a BSc in Management and a Diploma in Marketing from DIT. Beyond mastering core MBA modules—strategy, finance, operations, organizational behavior—I cut my teeth advising clients on mortgages and investment products during a six-month placement in financial services.

In 2000 I relocated to France to deepen my language skills, and by 2001 I was managing key accounts and trade-marketing programs for Brasseries Kronenbourg. A twelve-month stint in Australia followed, where I identified an opportunity to bring Anglo-Irish specialty beverages to the French market.

I then spent a year in business development for small wineries across the U.S. and Europe, and in 2006 launched my own direct-distribution network—growing it from a handful of SKUs to a €30M+ turnover over fifteen years.

When COVID-19 forced a rethink in 2020, I led a rapid pivot from B2B distribution to a consumer-facing e-commerce platform—complete with custom CRM, AI-driven forecasting agents, and dynamic marketing automation. Alongside that, I've upskilled in AI development, prompt engineering, and "vibe coding," ensuring my next chapter blends deep industry know-how with cutting-edge technology.

Having built my career across consulting, banking, and large-scale FMCG—spanning wine, beer, cider, spirits, and snack foods—I'm now pivoting into the AI-powered SaaS sector. Throughout each phase, I've combined rigorous theory with hands-on execution, bringing strategic insight, operational grit, and technical fluency to every growth-oriented challenge.
